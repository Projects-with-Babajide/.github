# Disposable by Default

**Two-week AI experiments for product, sales, marketing, and ops teams — built for clarity, not vendor lock-in.**

We run focused two-week sprints. Pick one real problem, build a working experiment, and learn what's actually possible. No platforms. No long roadmaps. Just results.

Most AI initiatives die in committee. Ours ship in 14 days. 🚀

---

### What we do

🎯 **AI Sprint Consulting** — We pinpoint high-leverage problems where AI makes a real difference, design a focused experiment with clear success criteria, and in two weeks you have something working and the clarity to make your next move.

🧑‍🏫 **Enterprise AI Training** — Hands-on workshops for engineering teams (200–500+ engineers) on AI adoption, workflow design, and tool integration. **2,000+ developers trained** and counting.

### What you walk away with

✅ **Working experiments** — actual prototypes you can demo to stakeholders, not slide decks

📊 **Success criteria** — clear metrics so you know if it's worth pursuing further

📄 **Documentation** — everything your team needs to extend it or deliberately sunset it

🧭 **Decision framework** — scale it, pivot, or kill it — no sunk-cost guilt
