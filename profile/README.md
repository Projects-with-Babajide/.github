# Disposable by Default

**Two-week AI experiments for product, sales, marketing, and ops teams — built for clarity, not vendor lock-in.**

We run focused two-week sprints. Pick one real problem, build a working experiment, and learn what's actually possible. No platforms. No long roadmaps. Just results.

### What we do

**AI Sprint Consulting** — Instead of year-long roadmaps and bloated contracts, we pinpoint high-leverage problems where AI makes a real difference, design a focused experiment with clear success criteria, and in two weeks you have something working and the clarity to make your next move.

**Enterprise AI Training** — Hands-on workshops for engineering teams (200-500+ engineers) on AI adoption, workflow design, and tool integration. Trained **2,000+ developers** on AI-assisted workflows.

### What you walk away with

- **Working experiments** — actual prototypes you can show to your team, test with users, or demo to stakeholders
- **Success criteria** — clear metrics to evaluate whether the approach is worth pursuing further
- **Documentation** — everything your team needs to understand, extend, or deliberately sunset the experiment
- **Decision framework** — scale it, pivot, or move on — without the sunk cost fallacy
